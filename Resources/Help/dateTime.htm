Date and Time

Random date button

Click on this to get a randomly selected date. By using the random date button you get to fly in all seasons!

Date picker field

Click on this to specify a date.

Random time button

Click on this to get a randomly selected time. By using the random time button you will get to experience flying at all times of the day, even at night!

Time picker field

Click on this to specify a time.

Note: Prepar3D sometimes does unexpected things for the time you select. For example a time you specify expecting dawn lighting might give you night lighting. If this happens you can simply change the time of day and the scenario will reload.
